BACKLOG & ROADMAP CONTINUITY DOCUMENT
[Project Name] | v[X.Y] | [Month Year]

Purpose: The "what's next" doc. Captures the prioritized work ahead --
epics, upcoming sprints, deferred items, and the technical debt registry.
Pin this in sprint planning sessions, roadmap reviews, and any LLM session
focused on prioritization or scope discussion.

FORMAT NOTE: Plain text (.txt). LLM-agnostic.

UPDATE CADENCE: Updated at sprint planning (mandatory), at backlog
grooming sessions, and whenever new work is identified or priorities
shift. Roadmap section updated at least once per quarter.
