SPRINT CONTINUITY DOCUMENT
[Project Name] | v[X.Y] | [Month Year]

Purpose: Rolling sprint state. Captures the current sprint's goals,
in-flight work by team member-role, blockers, daily standup deltas, and
carry-over from the prior sprint. Updated daily (or per-standup at minimum).
This is the doc that ages out fastest and gets pruned most aggressively.

FORMAT NOTE: Plain text (.txt). LLM-agnostic.

UPDATE CADENCE: At minimum, updated at the close of each daily standup.
Ideally, in-flight items are updated by their owners as state changes.
Sprint Review and Retro outputs are captured here before sprint close,
then the doc rolls into the next sprint.
